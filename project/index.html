About The App

Leaflr empowers the individual through visual quantification of personal impact. We have developed a mobile friendly website that allows users to calculate and visualize their personal impact on the environment. Users are encouraged to calculate, track and share their own conservation progress over various social networks. If you would like to learn more, reach out to us at hello@leaflr.com.
